Firecracker Salmon

Firecracker Salmon is a tantalizing dish that combines succulent salmon fillets with a fiery burst of flavor. The salmon is coated in a spicy marinade featuring ingredients like sriracha, soy sauce, brown sugar, and garlic, then grilled or baked to perfection. With its bold and zesty taste, this dish is sure to ignite your taste buds with delight.

Ingredients

    Salmon

  • 1.5 Pounds skinless salmon fillet
  • 2-3 Tablespoons canola or avacado oil
  • Chopped chives, for garnish

    • Marinade

  • 1/2-1/3 Cup Sriracha
  • 1/2-1/3 Cup brown sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 Cloves garlic grated
  • 1-2 Teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 1/2-1 Teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Cut salmon into 1&1/2 inch strips
  2. Mix marinade ingredients into a large bowl
  3. Marinate Salmon strips in refrigerator for 30-60 minutes
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  5. Heat oil over medium high heat in a large oven-safe skillet
  6. Sear salmon on both sides, about 2-3 minutes each side
  7. Transfer skillet to oven to cook an additional 5 minutes
  8. Serve over rice, top with chives
