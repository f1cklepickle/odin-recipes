Fish Tacos

This recipe features succulent cod fillets seasoned with taco spices, paired with a zesty Creamy Honey & Lime Sauce and a vibrant Sweet & Spicy Coleslaw. The fish is baked to perfection after being brushed with a buttery taco seasoning mix. The tangy sweetness of the sauce complements the crunchy coleslaw, creating a burst of flavors in every bite. Enjoy wrapped in tortillas or served over a rice bowl.

Ingredients

    Fish

  • 4 Frozen cod fillets
  • 2 Tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 Tablespoons taco seasoning

    • Creamy honey & lime sauce

  • 1/4 Cup sour cream
  • 1&1/2 Teaspoons lime juce
  • 1 Tablespoon milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 Teaspoon honey

    • Sweet & Spicy Coleslaw

  • 16oz coleslaw mix
  • 1/2 Cup diced red pepper
  • 1/2 Cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 Cup ranch
  • 2 Tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoons honey
  • 1-2 Tablespoons Sriracha
  • 1 Teaspoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

    Fish

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Mix butter and taco seasoning, brush on frozen fillets
  3. Bake for 25 minutes

    Creamy honey & lime sauce

  1. whisk together sour cream, lime juice, honey, milk, and salt

    Sweet & Spicy coleslaw

  1. Stir together coleslaw, red peppers, and cilanto
  2. whisk together ranch, vinegar, honey, sriracha, and lime juice
  3. Pour over coleslaw, salt and pepper to taste

    Serve

  • Fill tortilla with rice, fish, sweet & spicy coleslaw, sauce, avacado is optional
  • Serve over rice bowls for a delicous alternative
