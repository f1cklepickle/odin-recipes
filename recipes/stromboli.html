Stromboli

Stromboli is a delicious Italian-American creation, resembling a rolled pizza or sandwich. It features a thin layer of dough filled with a variety of ingredients such as Italian meats, cheeses, vegetables, and herbs. Once assembled, it's rolled into a log shape, baked until golden and crispy, then sliced into hearty portions. Stromboli offers a delightful combination of flavors and textures, making it a satisfying meal or snack option.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza crust dough
  • 1 Package pepperoni
  • 15oz Tomato sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon oregano
  • 1 Teaspoon onion flakes
  • 2 Teaspoons sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 Teaspoon pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon basil
  • 8oz Mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Roll dough out on wax paper into rectangle
  3. Combine tomato sauce, oregano, onion flakes, sugar, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and basil in bowl
  4. Spread mixture on dough, save some sauce for dipping
  5. Add pepperoni, 4oz cheese, and any other desired toppings
  6. Top with 4oz cheese
  7. Roll dough from long side into log shape
  8. Crimp ends and smooth dough seams
  9. Cut slits across top of dough to cook through
  10. Remove wax paper
  11. Bake at 400 degrees F for 15-20 minutes
