Korean Beef

Korean beef is a savory and slightly sweet dish featuring ground beef marinated in a blend of soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and sugar. It's a flavorful meat that's enjoyed with rice and various accompaniments.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound ground beef
  • 3 Cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 Cup brown sugar, add more if you like it sweeter
  • 1/4 Cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 Teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • A pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1/2 Cup sliced green onions

Instructions

  1. Brown beef in a large skillet
  2. Once the beef is nearly done, add garlic and cook for 2 more minutes
  3. Drain most fat from the beef
  4. Add brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, salt, pepper, and red pepper
  5. Set to medium heat and simmer for about 3 minutes
  6. Serve over rice, top with green onion
